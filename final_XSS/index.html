Welcome!!!

XSS, a common web vulnerability, allows attackers to inject malicious scripts into web pages, leading to potentially harmful consequences. By mastering XSS, you'll gain insight into the techniques used by hackers and acquire the knowledge needed to protect web applications from such attacks.

Throughout this challenge, you'll explore various scenarios where XSS can be exploited. Imagine being able to:

  • Steal Cookies: Learn how to inject malicious scripts into vulnerable websites to steal user session cookies. These cookies can grant unauthorized access to user accounts and provide an attacker with control over sensitive information.
  • Craft Phishing Attacks: Discover how XSS can be used to create convincing phishing websites. Experience firsthand how users can unknowingly fall victim to these attacks, compromising their personal data.
  • Redirect Users: Explore the art of redirection through XSS. Inject scripts that cleverly reroute unsuspecting users to malicious websites, tricking them into revealing their credentials or falling into other traps.
  • Deface Websites: Embrace the power of XSS to modify the content of vulnerable websites. Imagine the impact of replacing legitimate information with unexpected, sometimes humorous, and sometimes dangerous content.

By actively engaging with these challenges, you'll develop a deep understanding of XSS and its implications. You'll also gain practical experience in implementing input validation and output encoding techniques to fortify web applications against such attacks.
